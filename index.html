The Services We Provide

Amazing Care provides in-home care services that range from companion and homemaking services to personal Care. Our mission is to promote quality of life and maintain independence, security and comfort to all our clients. Our services are Be Safe at Home, Memory Care, Respite Care and Dial my Ride.

About Us

AMAZING CARE, a licensed and insured In-Home Care agency, specializes in providing personalized in-home care plans specifically designed to address the many challenges our clients face.

We understand your frustrations when life has changed your loved ones ability to live independently.
